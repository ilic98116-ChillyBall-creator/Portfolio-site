About Me

Welcome! I'm Ana and I LOVE narrative design. My skills adapt from coding fundementals to the presentation of buisness and creative design. My passion stemmed in highschool when I took IB art without the required credentials from grade 9 and 10; however, I had the confidence in myself to display my efforts in sketching, sculpting and animation. Later in my academic career I was introduced to coding where my passion for gaming since childhood became a digital reality between my finger tips. Beyond that I discovered the arts of film and photography and developed a keen sense for close-up photography and landscape admiring the beauty of both architecture and natural beauty. And finally economics, previously in highschool I took IB econ as a mandatory course but in university when I encountered Andrew Gibson, he inspired me with a deep passion of how our society is constructed and developing throughout our presence from every willingness to pay as he once called it "girl math".
